# Project Intro

## Chinese

AI News Open 是一个面向开源发布的 AI 新闻聚合工具，目标是每天自动抓取国内外 AI 新闻，并把分散的信息源整理成可运营、可检索、可发布的中文日报工作流。它覆盖采集、清洗、去重、正文提取、国际新闻中文翻译、日报生成、历史存档和多渠道发布，既可以作为个人项目运行，也可以作为轻量化内容基础设施使用。

## English

AI News Open is an open-source AI news aggregation toolkit designed to turn scattered domestic and international AI coverage into a practical Chinese daily digest workflow. It covers ingestion, cleanup, deduplication, content extraction, Chinese translation for global stories, digest generation, archive management, and multi-channel publishing. It can be used as a personal automation project or as lightweight infrastructure for AI-focused content operations.
